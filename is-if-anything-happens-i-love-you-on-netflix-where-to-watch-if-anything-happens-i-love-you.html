Is If Anything Happens I Love You on Netflix? Where To Watch If Anything Happens I Love You

Sunday night delivered the 2021 Academy Awards, which means Monday morning is the perfect time to catch up on a few Oscar winners.

Chloé Zhao’s Nomadland was the big winner of the night as the film took home Best Picture, Best Director (Zhao), and Best Actress (Frances McDormand). But if you’re looking to stream a few Oscar winners, you may want to start with If Anything Happens I Love You on Netflix.

Winning the Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film, the movie centers on two grieving parents who journey through an emotional void as they mourn the loss of a child following a deadly school shooting. Written and directed by Will McCormack and Michael Govier, the critically-acclaimed film boasts a perfect Rotten Tomatoes score, with Decider’s Anna Menta calling it “one of the year’s most powerful pieces of art.”

How can you watch If Anything Happens I Love You online? Here’s everything you need to know.

IS IF ANYTHING HAPPENS I LOVE YOU ON NETFLIX?

Yes! Clocking in at just under 13 minutes, If Anything Happens I Love You is currently available to stream on Netflix.

IS IF ANYTHING HAPPENS I LOVE YOU ON HULU?

Nope.

WHERE TO WATCH IF ANYTHING HAPPENS I LOVE YOU ONLINE:

Unfortunately, If Anything Happens I Love You isn’t currently available to rent so Netflix is your only option.

WHERE CAN I WATCH ALL THE 2021 OSCAR WINNERS ONLINE?

If you’re looking to watch Nomadland, Hulu’s your best bet, while Sound of Metal is available on Prime Video. A complete list of where to stream all the 2021 Oscar winners is available on Decider.
