Jerwin Juntilla Ancajas is a Filipino professional boxer and athlete. Jerwin Juntilla Ancajas held the IBF junior bantamweight title between September 2016 to February 2022.

Jerwin was introduced to boxing in the second grade by his brother, Jesar Ancajas.

Professional Life and Career

Jerwin made his professional debut in July 2009. Therefore, he won his first major title; the WBO Asia Pacific Youth junior-bantamweight title against Rex Tito scheduled in SM City Lipa. He suffered a loss in his 15th pro bout, losing a majority decision to Mark Anthony Geraldo.

On September 3, 2016, he became the IBF junior bantamweight champion when he defeated McJoe Arroyo. Jerwin made his first defense on January 29, 2017, beating José Alfredo Rodríguez by technical knockout.

He defeated Jamie Conlan with around 6 TKO on November 18, 2017.

In December, it was announced that this boxer had signed with Top Rank and would make his U.S. debut in February 2018. And, finally, on 3 February 2018, he would successfully defend his IBF belt in his US debut.

Likewise, in his next title defense, he faced IBF #1 ranked title contender Jonas Sultan. However, he won the fight comfortably.
