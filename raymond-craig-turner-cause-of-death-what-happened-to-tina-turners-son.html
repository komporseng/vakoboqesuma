What happened to Tina Turner's son?

Tina Turner is a Swiss singer and actress who was born in the United States in November of 1939 to  Zelma Priscilla and Floyd Richard Bullock. Her parents named her Anna Mae Bullock.

Bullock had two older sisters, Evelyn Juanita Currie and Ruby Alline Bullock who was a songwriter. She is also the first cousin once removed of bluesman Eugene Bridges.

She gained notoriety as Ike and Tina Turner’s lead singer before beginning a lucrative solo career, earning her the title “Queen of Rock ‘n’ Roll.” She has won many awards and accolades for her performances.
