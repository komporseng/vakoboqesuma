HARDY and Caleigh Ryan are Married in Nashville Ceremony!

HARDY and Caleigh Ryan are married (source: People)

  • The Wait in the Truck singer HARDY and his fiance Caleigh Ryan are married. 
  • The couple of 4 years have happily walked down the aisle on October 29, 2022.
  • Learn about the relationship timeline of HARDY and Caleigh Ryan.

American country music singer HARDY has tied the knot with his lady love at Diamond Creek Farms in Nashville!

HARDY and Caleigh Ryan are married!

Caleigh Ryan and Hardy exchanged their romantic vows after dating for four long years in Nashville on October 29, 2022. They chose a custom-designed by Naama & Anat as their wedding attire whose outfit impressed them,

“She did a fabulous job. I love it. It came out so beautifully, and I’m so excited about it. We were playing around with the idea of a second dress, but I just love my gown so much that I want to wear it the whole time.”

The influencer donned her wedding look with makeup by Halee Brooke and hair by Jessica Miller. The groom styled a suit by STITCHED in Las Vegas accessorizing it with a bow tie and custom gold cuff links that belonged to his late great-uncle.

Talking about their marriage planned by Grey Boroff of House of Grey Events, she told,

“It’s like huge, green pastures. It’s a beautiful stone venue. It has an outside ceremony space that looks out to all the trees and Tennessee landscape. It’ll be moody.”

Wedding guests and dinner of Caleigh Ryan

Their romantic nuptial ceremony was memorable as the couple had a cocktail hour featuring mini donkeys who cart around beer in baskets to guests. The couple had their first dance on “Faithfully” by Journey.

Wedding guests like country music friends Dierks Bentley, Brantley Gilbert, Lauren Alaina, Devin Dawson, Mitchell Tenpenny, and Lindsay Ell. Also, Travis Denning, RaeLynn, ERNEST, Renee Blair, Jameson Rodgers, and Morgan Wallen were enjoying the party.

Meanwhile, the fancy thing about the wedding was that the guests had the option to get a 10-minute love or wedding-themed tattoo by Mike Stoll.

Caleigh Ryan paid homage to HARDY’s grandmother when it comes to the wedding cake. They choose a layered caramel cake by Dessert Designs by Leland. Wedding late-night snacks included hot chicken sandwiches and tater-tot nachos.

Relationship Timeline of HARDY and Caleigh Ryan

HARDY and Caleigh Ryan are dating since 2018 after encountering each other once Hardy slid into Ryan’s DMs on Instagram. The singer of his early dating life and Arizona road trip told,

“Two months after we started dating, we were in Arizona on a road trip, and we played that song. We put it on, and I teared up going down the road. I got choked up for like 30 seconds. “

Give Heaven Some Hell singer proposed to his longtime girlfriend, now wife at the Lyric Oxford in Mississippi in August 2021. They’re now married in October 2022 after a year of engagement.
