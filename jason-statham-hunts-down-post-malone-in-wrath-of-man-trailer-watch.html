Jason Statham Hunts Down Post Malone in 'Wrath of Man' Trailer - Watch!

Post Malone lets out a cry while trying to escape Jason Statham in the first trailer for Wrath of Man.

Here’s a summary of the upcoming action movie: A mysterious and wild-eyed new cash truck security guard (Statham) surprises his coworkers during a heist in which he unexpectedly unleashes precision skills.

The crew is left wondering who he is and where he came from. Soon, the marksman’s ultimate motive becomes clear as he takes dramatic and irrevocable steps to settle a score.

Guy Ritchie directed the flick, which also stars Josh Hartnett, Scott Eastwood, Holt McCallany, Jeffrey Donovan, Laz Alonso, Raul Castillo, and Niamh Algar.

This marks Post Malone‘s second acting role, following an appearance on Spenser Confidential.

Wrath of Man is set to debut on May 7.
