Who is Berner?

Berner is a rapper and entrepreneur. Currently, he is associated with Wiz Khalifa’s recording label Taylor Gang Entertainment.

Berner- Birth, Age, Parents, Ethnicity

Berner was born on the Mansour Bahman Shahsavari on the 27th of October 1981, in San Francisco, California, United States.

His father was a Mexican chef at a restaurant on Fillmore Street. Also, his mother was an office worker.

His family moved to Arizona to open a restaurant. They are of Mexican ancestry.
