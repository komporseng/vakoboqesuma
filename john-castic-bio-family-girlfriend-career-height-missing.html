Who Is John Castic? His Family, Girlfriend, Career, Height, Missing

It is unhappy news we are here to discuss. We are here to tell you all that you have been wondering about John Castic who went missing early Saturday morning of 29 July 2023 after attending a concert at The Brooklyn Mirage. The disappearance has left everyone absolutely very worried about him because this is the same Big Apple venue another man tried to get into before he was found dead several weeks ago.

So, what happened? Let’s find out in this writing called ‘John Castic Bio’.

John Castic Missing

As of now, i.e., early August 2023, a search is still underway for John Castic, who vanished in the early morning hours of the said day after attending a Zeds Dead concert in New York City.

John, as they say, was last seen at approximately 3 a.m., according to a missing person flier. Also, he was seen wearing a white and navy blue floral shirt with khaki pants.

His friend Sara Kostecka was the one to issue a public plea on social media, urging people to come forward to help locate him. “Any information would be greatly appreciated, as well as sharing this with anyone in NYC that might have seen him”, she wrote in a Facebook post.

John is the second person to go missing in recent weeks after going to The Brooklyn Mirage.

Jonah Shales, another friend of Hohn’s, told Fox News Digital that his friend’s phone died after leaving the venue and has remained off ever since. He told the outlet no one has had any contact with him.

As for Goldman Sachs, John’s employer, in its statement shared to E! News on 1 August 2023 cited, their Office of Global Security is working closely with the NYPD to help locate John. They claimed they are in touch with his family during this difficult time and the investigation remains ongoing.

John Castic Career

Per LinkedIn, John Castic had been working full-time as a senior analyst at Goldman Sachs in Jersey City, New Jersey since August 2022.

For six months, before this, he was an account representative at Haver Analytics in New York, New York.

Even prior, at Alvarez & Marsal, also of New York, New York, he was a valuation associate from July 2020 until January 2022.

John first got his Associate of Arts – AAA from Harper in 2018. And then he studied for his Bachelor’s degree, Finance Honors at DePaul University between 2018 and 2020.

How Old Is John Castic?

John Castic was born in 1995. So, he was 27 years old only when he went missing.

John Castic Family

John’s mother is Dawn L Castic of Elk Grove Village, Illinois. She reached the age of 61 in December 2022. Per LinkedIn, she has been working as an administrative assistant/project coordinator at MasTec Network Solutions since January 2019. Before this, she worked at AT&T. Between 1981 and 1984, she went to Harper College for Associate Applied Science Degree in Dietetics.

John’s father Jeffery E Castic turned 58 in May 2023. The patriarch has not had a good reputation. Back in January 2011, he had been sentenced to nearly nine years in federal prison on charges he possessed child pornography on his home computer and distributed several images online.

U.S. District Court Judge had ordered then 45-year-old Jeffery imprisoned for 106 months on charges. Jeffery indeed admitted to charges of distributing and possessing child pornography, but his defense also sought a term of probation or a shorter prison term. Of course, the prosecutors rejected the argument.

In his family, John also has a brother named Joshua Castic.

Also, we discovered that John’s paternal grandparents are William A. Castic and Joy Vanda Castic. Both have passed away.

John’s grandmother grew up in Chicago during the Depression. She graduated from Schurz High School and attended Wright College where she met William and married him. They were married on 18 May 1957. Joy, after raising three children, went back to school and graduated from Elmhust College and then earned a Master’s degree also. She then pursued teaching as a career because she wanted to make a difference. Later in life, she retired after being disabled in a car accident that took the life of her husband.
